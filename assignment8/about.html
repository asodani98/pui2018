Hi, my name is Anurati Sodani and this is my travel website. I created this to serve as a diary for what I have learned on my travels and what I would like to share with the rest of the world. I started traveling at a young age and it always meant a lot to me.

Every place I visited I learned something new about a culture, types of people or even myself. Hence, I made it my mission to document in both journals and pictures to remember everything I had experienced on these trips. I want to continue these explorations and have a long list of places I want to be able to add it to my website.
